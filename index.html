What is Parklopedia?

Parklopedia allows you to search and access information about National Parks directly from the publically available National Park Service API!

This means you can...

Search by State name to find available National Parks.

Select a Park to navigate to a page dedicated to your selected Park with more information.

Use the current and three day forecast to see what the weather status is for the park.

Use the Alerts section to see if there are any alerts or closures for the selected park.

See if specific amenities are listed as available at your selected park.
